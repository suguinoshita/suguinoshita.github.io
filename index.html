Weight comparison: – Volume = Area x Length
– Mass = ρ x Area x Length
*constant section and homogeneous material.

Stiffness comparison: – Axial stiffness = E.A/L
– Bending stiffness = E.Ixx/L or E.Iyy/L
– Torsional stiffness = G.Izz/L

Stress comparison: – Axial stress: σ = F/A
– Bending stress over x-axis: σ = Mx/Sx,min
– Bending stress over y-axis: σ = My/Sy,min
