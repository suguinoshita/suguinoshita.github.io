About BeamSectionCalc

   BeamSectionCalc is a free online tool developed from engineers to engineers - or whomever else this may be useful!
   Here you can find a fast, clear and reliable platform to not only calculate a variety of cross-section properties, but also to compare different designs side-to-side and check which design changes will be the most assertive for your projects! Our goal is to help you to make the best decisions always.
