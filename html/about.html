About BeamSectionCalc

   BeamSectionCalc is a free online tool developed from engineers to engineers - or whomever else this may be useful!
   Here you can find a fast, clear and reliable platform to not only calculate a variety of cross-section properties, but also to compare different designs side-to-side and check which design change will be the most assertive for your projects! Our goal is to help you to make the best decisions always.

Calculate and compare cross-sections

Compare different designs and choose the best solution!
Start by selecting one or more cross-sections to be computed - for instance, if you want to compare two or more options for your project.

In the plots, you can verify which option is lighter, stiffer, or will show lower stresses under the same loads (assuming simple traction load or simple bending moment on each axis, and same beam lengths and homogeneous material).

You can change the dimensions or section types at anytime and all the results will be updated instantly! You may also choose which section is to be used as reference in the plots!

Sensitivity Analysis

Visualize how each dimension affects the beam properties and adjust your design efficiently!

You may ask yourself: which dimension should be modified to lower the stresses in an efficient way?
or
which dimension can I change to reduce mass, but with minimal impact in the bending stiffness?

This page can help you!

Start by selecting one cross-sections to be computed.
In the plots, you can verify which dimension has the higher (or lower) effect on the beam mass, stiffness and stresses!
*again, assuming simple traction load or simple bending moment on each axis, and same beam lengths and homogeneous material.
